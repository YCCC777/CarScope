How to use

  1. Go to a CarGurus vehicle listing page
  2. Click the CarScope icon in your browser toolbar
  3. View recalls, complaints, and VIN details in the Vehicle tab
  4. Click Save to track the car and monitor its price
  5. Use the Saved tab to manage your shortlist, notes, and ratings
  6. Check off items in the Pre-Purchase Checklist before visiting a dealer
  7. Select cars and switch to the Compare tab for side-by-side specs
Report an issue / Send feedback

Data sources

⚠️ NHTSA Recalls

Source: api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle. Free, no API key required. Returns open safety recalls by make / model / year. US coverage only.

💬 NHTSA Complaints

Source: api.nhtsa.gov/complaints/complaintsByVehicle. User-submitted safety complaints. CarScope flags crash / fire / injury mentions to highlight the most serious issues.

🔑 VIN Decode

Source: vpic.nhtsa.dot.gov (NHTSA vPIC API). Decodes any 17-character VIN to manufacturer specs: plant, engine, series, and more. Free, no API key required.

📊 Price Tracking

Local only. CarScope records the listed price every time you visit a saved car. Price history is stored in your browser and never uploaded.

Background refresh runs every 24 hours · stores up to 10 price entries per car

All data is from public sources. CarScope does not store any personal information and does not upload anything to any server.
